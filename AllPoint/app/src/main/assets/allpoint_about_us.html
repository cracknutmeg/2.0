The Allpoint Network® locator app provides locations for the 55,000 surcharge-free ATMs in the Allpoint Network. To gain surcharge-free access to these ATMs, you must be a cardholder of a financial institution that participates in Allpoint.

Want to know if your institution participates in Allpoint? Contact us today at info@allpointnetwork.com.

Features:
- Easily locate Allpoint’s 55,000 surcharge-free ATMs across the US, Canada, UK, Australia and Mexico.
- Access the most accurate and updated location data.
- Search by current location, city and state, or zip code.
- Scroll a list of the closest locations or view an overview map.
- Retrieve step-by-step directions* to your selected location.

*Maps and directions provided by Google.
Only authorized Allpoint Network locators carry the Allpoint surcharge-free guarantee.
